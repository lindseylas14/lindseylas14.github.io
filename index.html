Book Review By: Lindsey Stephens

Romeo and Juliet

Description

Romeo and Juliet is a fascinating story of two people with a forbidden love for each other. It is a dramatic tale of feuding families, love, and loss. Throughout the story, we meet enticing characters with different backgrounds and motives that make the story into the classic that it has become. The two main characters, Romeo and Juliet, show us what it means to be young and in love, with what seems like the whole world against them. Overall, it is a great story that everyone should read because of its underlying message of love. I recommend it 1010 times over because this classic is as necessary as H2O.
